FabricXai is an AI-powered platform built for the RMG (Ready-Made Garments) industry. It provides BRM Intelligence and Production Intelligence solutions designed for garment factories and buying houses.

What is GarmentsOS and how does it work?
GarmentsOS is an AI-powered operating system built for garment factories and buying houses. It digitizes buyer workflow, order tracking, and production monitoring in one platform. It automates lead management, techpack parsing, costing, production line tracking, and compliance, connecting teams and buyers in real time.

How can digital transformation help a manufacturing facility?
It reduces delays, cuts costs, improves productivity, and meets buyer expectations with real-time visibility and automation. Factories using solutions like FabricXai have reduced lead times by 30–50% and cut follow-up costs by ~35%.

What modules are included?
BRM (Buyer Relationship Management), Production Intelligence, Compliance, Finance, Workforce, and Smart Reporting. Workflows include techpack parsing, costing approvals, line tracking, buyer follow-ups, attendance, wage reports, and AI forecasting.

Do you integrate with existing systems?
Yes. Integrations supported include Tally, SAP, QuickBooks, Odoo, DHL and more via API/webhook, with one-way or two-way sync.

Security & compliance
Role-based access, audit logs, and readiness for GDPR, ISO 27001, and ILO standards. Private cloud and on‑prem options are available.

Implementation
Typical onboarding in 10–21 days. Phased rollout: BRM first, then production modules. Includes sandbox, guided setup, and role-based training.

Results & ROI examples
Factories see 8–12% gains in on-time delivery and improved buyer retention. Examples include significant savings from reduced overhead and faster lead times.
